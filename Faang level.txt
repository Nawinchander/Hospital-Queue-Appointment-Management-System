FAANG INTERVIEW QUESTIONS
Why Redis?

To reduce:

database hits
response latency

Redis is memory-based and extremely fast.

Why BullMQ?

Notification sending:

should not block APIs

BullMQ provides:

retries
async processing
job persistence

Why JWT?

Stateless authentication:

scalable
no server session needed
Why WebSockets?

Queue updates are real-time.

Polling wastes:

bandwidth
server resources
