Why Modularize Sockets?

Because in large apps:

different domains
different events
easier maintenance
microservice friendly
Interview Questions
Why Separate Socket Service?

Because:

avoids duplicate connections
improves maintainability
reusable architecture

Why Use Singleton Socket Instance?

Multiple socket instances:

increase memory
duplicate events
create scaling problems

Singleton avoids this.

Why Socket Manager on Backend?

To avoid:

circular imports
tightly coupled architecture

Also improves:

scalability
modularity
testing
